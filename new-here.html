Documentary Upcoming Producer

A hybrid documentary that follows a pizza delivery driver as she falls down a virtual rabbit hole into a world of artists and cypherpunks working to build a more equitable internet.

This film is being produced with a mix of live-action, Unreal Engine animation, hand-drawn animation, 2D digital animation, and AI generated media.

Credits

  • Produced by Shane Boris, Will Swann, Lauren Guido, Dan Sickles, Hannah Roodman, & Bruce Thierry Cheung
